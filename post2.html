In Loving Memory of Breonna Taylor

A Portrait in Voronoi Stippling

A while ago, I was perusing through Google and I found a library called D3.js in which you can create various types of data visualizations.

What is Voronoi Stippling?

Voronoi stippling is a process of data visualization in which points are initially positioned randomly using rejection sampling. At each iteration, Voronoi cell centroids are weighted by the lightness of the contained pixels. The stippling implemented in Breonna Taylor's portrait applies a weighted variant of Lloyd's algorithm. Lloyd's algorithm repeatedly moves each point toward its associated Voronoi cell's centroid.

Who is Breonna Taylor?

Breonna Taylor was a 26-year-old EMT who was helping with the COVID pandemic. On the evening in which she was sleeping in her apartment, three officers fired 22 shots because they had mistaken her for the individual that they were supposed to arrest.
